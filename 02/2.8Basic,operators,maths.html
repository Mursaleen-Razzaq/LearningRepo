Operators in JavaScript

In mathematics, basic operators are symbols used to perform simple calculations on numbers.

1. Addition (+) combines two or more numbers (e.g., 45 + 25 = 70).
2. Subtraction (−) finds the difference between numbers (e.g., 45 − 25 = 20).
3. Multiplication (× or *) repeats addition of a number (e.g., 45 × 25 = 1125).
4. Division (÷ or /) splits a number into equal parts (e.g., 45 ÷ 25 = 1.8).
5. Modulus (%) gives the remainder after division (e.g., 45 % 25 = 20).
These operators are the foundation of arithmetic and are used in almost all mathematical calculations.
